Project Overview

This project focused on building an interactive sales and profit analysis dashboard for a retail store using Superstore data. The objective was to provide insights into sales performance across various product categories, regions, and timeframes.

Scope of Work

  • Connected and cleaned Superstore datasets to ensure consistency.
  • Created DimCustomer, DimProduct, DimGeographique, and DimDate tables, establishing one-to-many relationships with the fact table.
  • Addressed data challenges such as non-unique ProductID, CustomerID, and Zipcode, by introducing surrogate keys.
  • Built custom DAX measures to calculate KPIs such as Total Sales, Profit Margins, and Sales Growth Rate.
  • Designed an interactive dashboard with features like: - Dynamic slicers for filtering by region, product category, and customer segments. - Drill-through pages for deeper analysis of regional sales. - Trend lines and heatmaps to identify sales peaks and underperforming products.

Technologies Used

  • Power BI (Data modeling, visualization)
  • DAX formulas (Sales Growth %, Profitability Analysis)
  • Power Query for data cleaning
  • Relationships and surrogate keys for a normalized data model

Outcome & Impact

The dashboard empowered users to monitor their business performance in real-time, track key sales trends, and adjust strategies accordingly. The insights generated from the dashboard helped optimize product placement and promotional efforts, resulting in better profit margins and increased sales efficiency.
